Account & Data Deletion Request

This page provides instructions on how to request the deletion of your account and associated data for the AIMS mobile application, as required by the Google Play Store.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request that your account and all associated data be permanently deleted, please send an email to the following address:

fumeinomichino406@gmail.com

What to Include in Your Email:

To verify your request and process it promptly, please ensure your email includes the following:

  • Subject Line: Account Deletion Request for AIMS App
  • Body of the Email: The email address that you used to register your account in the AIMS app.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon receiving and verifying your request, we will permanently delete the following data associated with your account from our Firebase backend:

  • Your Firebase Authentication user record (which includes your UID, email address, and any linked sign-in providers).
  • Any other data directly tied to your user ID within our system.

Please note that anonymized usage and performance data collected by Google services (like Firebase Performance Monitoring or Analytics) may not be personally identifiable and may be retained in an aggregated form.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests will be processed within 7-14 business days of receiving a valid request. We will send a confirmation email to your registered email address once the deletion is complete.
